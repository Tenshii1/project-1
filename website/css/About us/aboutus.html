About us:

The Story of WcRonald's:



• The name WcRonald's represents a blend of tradition and innovation.
"Wc" stands for Welcoming Comfort, capturing the essence of what we aim to bring to every visitor—comfort, warmth,
and a sense of belonging. By incorporating "Ronald's," a name often associated with friendly familiarity and joy,
we emphasize our commitment to creating an inviting environment where everyone feels at home.

• It was inspired by a local friend, Ronald, who brought welcoming comfort to everyone he met.
Our founders wanted to capture that warmth, choosing "Wc" for Welcoming Comfort and adding "Ronald's" to honor his legacy.
Today, WcRonald's is a place where everyone feels at home, enjoying great food and making meaningful connections.



Founders:



• It was founded in 2024 by Cyrus Villanueva and Erich Richelle Joy A. Catalunia.
